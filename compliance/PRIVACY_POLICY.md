## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Keystroke Symphony ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information from users of our educational platform, with special attention to compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Information We Collect

### For All Users
- **Account Information:** Email address, password (encrypted), birthdate
- **Usage Data:** Learning progress, exercise completion, session duration
- **Technical Data:** Browser type, device information, IP address (for security only)

### For Users Under 13 (with Parental Consent)
- **Limited Profile Data:** First name only (optional), age range
- **Learning Progress:** Exercise scores, typing metrics (non-identifiable)
- **Parental Contact:** Parent/guardian email address (for consent only)

### What We DO NOT Collect from Children Under 13
- ❌ Full name or address
- ❌ Phone number
- ❌ Photos or videos
- ❌ Social media profiles
- ❌ Precise geolocation
- ❌ Persistent identifiers for behavioral advertising

## How We Use Information

- **Educational Purpose:** Track learning progress and provide personalized exercises
- **Account Management:** Authenticate users and manage subscriptions
- **Communication:** Send service updates and parental consent requests (not marketing)
- **Compliance:** Maintain audit logs for COPPA/FERPA compliance
- **Security:** Prevent fraud and protect against unauthorized access

## Parental Rights Under COPPA

Parents of children under 13 have the right to:

1. **Review** the personal information collected from their child
2. **Request deletion** of their child's personal information
3. **Revoke consent** and refuse further collection of information
4. **Opt out** of certain features (e.g., forum access, which is already disabled for under-13 users)

### How to Exercise Your Rights

**Review child data:**
Email privacy@keystrokesymphony.com with subject "Data Access Request"

**Delete child data:**
Visit [yourdomain.com/parent-dashboard](https://yourdomain.com/parent-dashboard) or email privacy@keystrokesymphony.com

**Revoke consent:**
Use the "Revoke Consent" button in your parent dashboard or email us

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Profile data archived for 30 days, then permanently deleted (except legal compliance data)
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 7 years for compliance purposes (de-identified after 1 year)

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT sell** user data to third parties.

We share data only with:
- **Service Providers:** Supabase (hosting), Stripe (payments), Resend (email) — all COPPA-compliant
- **Legal Compliance:** When required by law or to protect safety

## Security Measures

- ✅ End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- ✅ Row-level security (RLS) policies in database
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Access controls and audit logging
- ✅ Automatic session timeouts

## Age Verification

Users must provide their birthdate during signup. Users under 13 are automatically placed in a restricted account requiring parental consent before accessing features beyond the public demo.

## Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify parents via email at least 30 days before any material changes to our privacy practices affecting children under 13.
